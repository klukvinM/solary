About Solarian

Solarian is a legendary coin, created from a fragment of a fallen star. One side absorbed the light of the celestial body, the Solar side, radiating warmth, life, and hope. The other side, the Lunar side, darkened, piercing with its coldness, awakening ambitions and thirst for power.
The fate of the world depends on the balance between the Light and Dark sides of Solarian.
